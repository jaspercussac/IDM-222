The OLD MASTERS: REMBRANDT

Alongside his fellow painter Johannes Vermeer, Rembrandt is considered the Grand Master of the Dutch Golden Age. This reproduction is based on a study of Rembrandt's masterful handwork, expressed especially in his explorations in the art of printmaking. In the style of the Old Master, a portrait of the Cuban leader Fidel Castro was made.
